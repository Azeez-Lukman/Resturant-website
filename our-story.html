Our Story

We started with a simple idea: bring authentic Nigerian food to the heart of Cape Town. Our journey began with a love for bold flavors and a passion for sharing them with the world. Today, we’re more than just a restaurant—we’re a community of food lovers, united by the rich, spicy goodness of Nigerian cuisine.
